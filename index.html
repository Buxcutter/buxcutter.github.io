Dorjan Kraja, LMHC, CRC

Dorjan is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor and a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor.
He also has certificates in Marriage and Family Counseling as well as Integrative Mental Health.

Dorjan provides therapeutic services to individuals, couples, and families. He utilizes an eclectic
style with a foundation of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy to form the therapy around the individual or couple.

Dorjan has been in the healthcare field since 2004 with experience working in hospitals, community agencies,
and private practice.

“I love helping others! I would love to help you in your journey to improved mental health, improved relationships,
and overall increased life satisfaction. I look at the client-counselor relationship as a partnership where we work
together to combine my education and clinical skills with your life experience and problem solving to achieve the goals
you set forth.

I am the expert in mental health, and you are the expert in your life. When we work together, we can identify real goals
with real directions and wort toward improving relationships, symptoms, and mood.”
